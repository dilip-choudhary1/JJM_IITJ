Events • Sep 24th '22

Inauguration of Center for Sustainable Drinking Water

On 24th September 2022 (Saturday), Shri Gajendra Singh Shekhawat, Hon’ble Union Minister of Jal Shakti, Government of India, inaugurated the ‘Center for Sustainable Drinking Water’ at IIT Jodhpur in the august presence of Professor Santanu Choudhury (Director IITJ), Professor S. R. Vadera (Dy. Director IITJ) and Dr. G. S. Toteja (CEO, JCKIF). The mandate of the center is capacity building, outreach, research and education (CORE) in the thematic area. Shri Amit Shukla, Director JJM was also present during the event.

Events • Feb 14th '23

Principal Scientific Advisor Officer Visit

On February 14, 2023; - a high-power team from the Office of the Principal Scientific Advisor visited the centre to get first-hand knowledge of the ongoing work and projects that are being carried out. During their visit, they were given demonstration of the models and technologies being researched and implemented in the field. The team had the opportunity to observe the practical applications of our research in areas such as sustainable energy, waste management, and water conservation. The demos brought out the efficacy of the models and technologies, providing the team with significant insights into the work being carried out and its potential for broader applications. The visit provided an opportunity for the team to interact with our students, researchers and experts; - and having a deeper understanding of our approach to research and development.
