Associate Faculties

Dr. Vikky Anand

Dr. Vikky Anand is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Chemical Engineering, Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur. He completed his B.Tech. in Chemical Engineering, National Institute of Technology Srinagar in 2013 and his M.Tech. in Chemical Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee in 2015. He also completed his Ph.D in Chemical Engineering (Electrohydrodynamics) at Indian Institute of Technology Bombay in 2019.

Email : vikky@iitj.ac.in

Dr. Abhilasha Maheshwari

Dr. Abhilasha Maheshwari is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Chemical Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur. She holds a Ph.D. in Chemical Engineering, which she completed from the Indian Institute of Technology Bombay in 2020. With a strong academic foundation, Dr. Maheshwari also has experience as a Project Scientist at the Industrial Research and Consultancy Centre (IIT Bombay). Her professional journey includes contributions in academia and research, making her a dedicated and knowledgeable expert in her field.

Email : abhilasham@iitj.ac.in

Post Doctoral Research Fellow

Naveen Kumar Chaturvedi

Naveen Kumar Chaturvedi is a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the Centre for Sustainable Drinking Water. He obtained his PhD from the National Institute of Technology Hamirpur. His research interests encompass advanced wastewater treatment, industrial wastewater treatment, desalination, and emerging contaminants. Naveen is actively engaged in research in these areas and has contributed significantly to the field.

Email : nchaturvedi@iitj.ac.in
