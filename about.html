About us

About us

Vision

The vision of the project is to establish a 'Demonstration Centre for Smart Graded-Water Supply Grid' utilizing IoT technology, sensors, and smart meters to create an intelligent water supply system at IIT Jodhpur campus.

This aims to optimize water usage, maintain water quality, and enhance distribution efficiency through intelligent automation and predictive modeling.

Mission

Mission

Smart Water Supply Grid: Develop an intelligent water supply system using sensors, meters, and IoT technology to monitor and manage water resources efficiently.

Clean Air and Emission Management: Collaborate with local authorities to assess air quality, emissions, and carrying capacity in urban areas, especially Kota city, for better environmental management.

Hazardous Waste Management: Verify and ensure compliance of hazardous waste generation and management practices through annual inventory checks, promoting adherence to regulatory guidelines.

Circular Economy in Wastewater Treatment: Transform the conventional wastewater treatment process into a circular economy model, emphasizing resource efficiency, waste reduction, and recycling of treated wastewater.

Algae-based Carbon Capture: Investigate the potential of using algae to capture carbon dioxide from exhaust flue gases, contributing to carbon capture and sustainable environmental practices.

Community Empowerment: Develop and implement decentralized clean water solutions, such as clay ceramic filters and advanced membrane-based systems, in rural and remote areas to provide safe drinking water and empower communities.

Collaboration and Research: Forge collaborations with academic institutions, industries, government bodies, and international partners to conduct research, share ideas, and develop innovative solutions for various environmental challenges.

Technology Consulting: Provide expertise to improve sewage treatment plants' efficiency, ensure quality effluent, and enhance environmental conditions in local areas.

Broad Objectives

Utilization of local materials and local skillsets for water purification leading to development and deployment of G-Filters

Utilization of local materials and local skillsets for irrigation leading to development and deployment of Sub-Surface Porous Vessel (SSPV) technology

Design, installation, testing and commissioning of Ultra-Filtration (UF) Membrane Assisted Sorption based Water Purification Systems in rural village schools of jodhpur district, Rajasthan through CSR funding.

Pilot a smart graded-water supply grid setup to optimally utilize the capabilities of locally available water and energy resources while delivering graded water supply.

Providing help and technically support the local authorities in evaluation of inventory of industrial hazardous and non-hazardous solid waste, wastewater, emissions and carrying capacity as well as advising appropriate solutions for city on a regular basis.

Capacity building of state government officials and other stakeholders' consultancy.

Act as a resource centre for national missions.

Components

Efforts should be made to source funds from different sources/ programmes and convergence is the key Development of in-village piped water supply infrastructure to provide tap water connection to every rural household Development of reliable drinking water sources and/ or augmentation of existing sources to provide long-term sustainability of water supply system Wherever necessary, bulk water transfer, treatment plants and distribution network to cater to every rural household Technological interventions for removal of contaminants where water quality is an issue Retrofitting of completed and ongoing schemes to provide FHTCs at minimum service level of 55 lpcd; Greywater management Support activities, i.e. IEC, HRD, training, development of utilities, water quality laboratories, water quality testing & surveillance, R&D, knowledge centre, capacity building of communities, etc. Any other unforeseen challenges/ issues emerging due to natural disasters/ calamities which affect the goal of FHTC to every household by 2024, as per guidelines of Ministry of Finance on Flexi Funds
